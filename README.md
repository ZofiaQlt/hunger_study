# Hunger around the World 

<img width="377" alt="logo_FAO" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/67431758/229183405-38f276b9-ae44-478a-bbc8-608a2a85a84b.png">

The datasets come from the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO) : https://www.fao.org/faostat/fr/#data 

The FAO is the specialized agency of the United Nations leading international efforts towards the eradication of hunger. Its objective is to achieve food security for all and ensure regular access to high-quality alimentation. With 195 members - 194 countries and the European Union, FAO is active in 130 countries worldwide.

This project aims at raising awareness and enabling to better understand the magnitude of the pressing issue of hunger in the world and focuses on analyzing and visualizing data related to global food insecurity to gain insights into its causes, impacts, and potential interventions.
